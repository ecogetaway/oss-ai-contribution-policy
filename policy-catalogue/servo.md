## Verbatim excerpt
> "Contributions must not include content generated by large language models or other probabilistic tools, including but not limited to Copilot or ChatGPT. This policy covers code, documentation, pull requests, issues, comments, and any other contributions to the Servo project."

## Notes
The most fully-argued prohibition in the batch: four named rationales (maintainer burden, correctness/security, copyright, ethics) — servo is a browser engine, so the correctness/security argument is domain-specific and strong. v0.2 signals: (1) the `rationale` enum again; (2) *comments* as a covered asset class; (3) a `revisit` field — servo explicitly frames the ban as provisional.
