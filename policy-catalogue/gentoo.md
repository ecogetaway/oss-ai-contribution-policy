## Verbatim excerpt
> "It is expressly forbidden to contribute to Gentoo any content that has been created with the assistance of Natural Language Processing artificial intelligence tools. This motion can be revisited, should a case been made over such a tool that does not pose copyright, ethical and quality concerns."

## Notes
One of the earliest distribution-level bans (April 2024), decided by formal governance vote with three named rationales (copyright, quality, ethics). v0.2 signals: (1) the **policy-scope boundary** — banning AI in contributions while explicitly allowing AI software as packaged content — needs a schema home; (2) the revisit/conditions clause recurs across ban-side projects (see servo) and deserves a field.
